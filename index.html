Pepper Kala

  • Time:1hr_50mins
  • Serves: 5 person
Ingredients Quantity Price(In Kuwait)
Flour 2.5 cups 3 kwd
Yeast 1 tbs 0.5 kwd
Sugar 0.5 cups 1 kwd
Baking Powder 0.5 tbs 250 fils
Vegetable Oil 1 liter 4 kwd

Preparation

First Step

Put the yeats in a bowl of warm water, stir it,and let it sit while you mix together the dry ingredients.

After mixing the dry ingredients,pour the yeast into it and stir until it has a very even consistancy. Let the mixture rest in a warm environment for 45min-1hr.

Second Step

Put your oil on the fire, when its hot use a table spoon to put a mixture of raw kala into the oil one spoon at a time and in differnt positions,don't let it overlap.

Remove the Kala from the oil when it looks brown, let it cool and it will be ready for eating.

Bon Appetit!!!

Meat Pie

  • Time:1hr
  • Serves: 5-10 person(depends on the size)
Ingredients Quantity Price(In Kuwait)
Flour 2.5 cups 3 kwd
Ground meat 1 lbs 5 kwd
Baking Powder 0.5 tbs 250 fils
Egg 4 1 kwd
Salt 0.5 tbs 250 fils
Butter 0.5 lbs 500 fils

Preparation

First Step

Season the ground meat with onion, black pepper and other season that goes with your taste and let it cook until it's done.

Mix the dry ingredients,pour the eggs and melted butter into it and stir until it has a very even consistancy. Let the dough rest in the fridge for 45min

Second Step

When your dough is well rested,cut it into smaller sizes that will make it easy for you to roll and flatten you can, you can use a round plate to help you cut a circular shape place a spoonful of the meat in the center and wrap the dough around it.

Third Step

Pre heat your oven to 150 degrees, place you raw meat pies in a baking tray and put the pies in the oven to bake for 45mins-1hr until it has a brownish exterior

Bon Appetit!!!!

Grilled fish

  • Time:1hr
  • Serves: 5 person
Ingredients Quantity Price(In Kuwait)
Snapper fish 5 pcs 10 kwd
Fish season 1 pk 2 kwd
Black pepper 1 tbs 250 fils
Vegetables 5 types 5 kwd

Preparation

First Step

Clean fish and season it with salt, fish season and black pepper.

Cover it with a foil and let it rest in the fridge for 45min

Second Step

Slice vegetables to to thin slices and and set it aside for serving later

Third Step

Light your grill and start to grill your fish, grill for at least 20 mins until it has a delicious brownish exterior.

Bon Appetit!!!!
